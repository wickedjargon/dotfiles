# xmouseless

> A fast and simple way to control the mouse using the keyboard.
> Keyboard-driven cursor movement, speed modifications, and clicking.

- Move the mouse cursor (Left / Down / Up / Right):

`{{h}} | {{j}} | {{k}} | {{l}}`

- Click the left mouse button:

`{{Space}} | {{Enter}} | {{Left Arrow}}`

- Click the right mouse button:

`{{Right Arrow}}`

- Click the middle mouse button:

`{{Up Arrow}} | {{Down Arrow}}`

- Change cursor speed (hold while moving - precision / slow / fast / warp):

`{{a}} | {{s}} | {{d}} | {{f}}`

- Scroll vertically (Down / Up):

`{{n}}` / `{{p}}`

- Fast vertical scrolling (Down / Up):

`{{=}}` / `{{-}}`

- Scroll horizontally:

`{{9}}` (Right) / `{{0}}` (Left)

- Move mouse cursor away (snap to right edge) and exit:

`{{q}}`

- Exit xmouseless:

`{{Escape}}`
